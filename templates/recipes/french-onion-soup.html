How to prepare French Onion Soup

instructions

step 1

In a large covered saucepan, cook onions and garlic in butter over medium-low heat for 8-10 minutes or until tender and golden, stirring occasionally.

step 2

Add water, bouillon and Worcestershire sauce; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

step 3

Ladle hot soup into 6 ovenproof bowls. Top each with a piece of French bread.

step 4

Cut each slice of cheese in half and place both pieces over the bread. Broil until cheese melts. Serve immediately.

ingredients

1 garlic clove, minced

1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

6 slices French bread
